Frequently asked questions

How much beef ribs per person?
As a main, plan about 1.0 lb raw per adult before shrink (teens about 0.8 lb; children about 0.6 lb). We apply your cut style shrink (about 45 to 50 percent) to estimate total raw pounds to buy.
What do the cut styles mean?
Short or plate ribs are meatier; expect about 45 percent shrink. Back ribs shrink about 50 percent. The tool adjusts raw pounds using your choice.
Why no average rack weight?
To stay universal. We output raw pounds. If you need rack counts, divide by your per-rack weight later.
How do appetite and event length change amounts?
Hearty groups and longer events eat more. Appetite, event length, and leftovers (+10% or +20%) tune the plan so you do not run short.
How does pricing work?
Subtotal is rounded raw pounds x price per lb. Then we add your tax and any fees for an estimated total and cost per person.
